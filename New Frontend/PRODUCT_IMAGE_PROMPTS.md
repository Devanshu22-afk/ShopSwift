# Product Image Generation Prompts for ShopSwift

## Image Specifications
- **Aspect Ratio**: Square (1:1) or 4:3 ratio
- **Dimensions**: 800x800px or 1200x1200px (minimum)
- **Background**: White or light neutral background (standard for e-commerce)
- **Style**: Professional product photography, clean, commercial
- **Format**: High resolution, suitable for web display

## General Tips for All Product Images:

1. **Background**: Always use pure white (#FFFFFF) or very light neutral background
2. **Lighting**: Soft, even lighting with subtle shadows for depth
3. **Composition**: Center the product, leave some breathing room around edges
4. **Quality**: High resolution, sharp focus on the product
5. **Style**: Consistent commercial product photography style
6. **No Text**: Don't include any text, labels, or watermarks on the product image itself
7. **Angles**: Show products at 45-degree angles when possible to display multiple sides
8. **Shadows**: Use subtle drop shadows for depth (not harsh shadows)

## After Generating Images:
1. Save images with descriptive names like: `product-smartphone.jpg`, `product-laptop.jpg`, etc.
2. Images will be uploaded to your Spring Boot backend when adding products
3. The backend will store them and serve them as base64 encoded images
