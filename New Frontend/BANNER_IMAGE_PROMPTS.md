# Banner Image Generation Prompts for ShopSwift

## Image Specifications
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 or 1920x1080 pixels (landscape)
- **Style**: Modern, clean, professional e-commerce banner
- **Format**: High resolution, suitable for web display

---

## Banner 1: "Outware Picks" / "Outdoor Picks"
**Gemini Prompt:**
```
Create a professional e-commerce banner image for an online shopping website called "ShopSwift". 
The image should feature outdoor and adventure products like hiking gear, camping equipment, 
backpacks, and outdoor apparel arranged attractively. Use a natural outdoor setting as background 
with mountains or forest in soft focus. The image should have a modern, clean aesthetic with 
vibrant but not overwhelming colors. Include space on the left side (about 30% of the image) 
for text overlay. The overall mood should be adventurous and energetic. Style: professional 
product photography, well-lit, high quality, commercial e-commerce banner.
```

**Alternative Shorter Prompt:**
```
E-commerce banner: Outdoor adventure products (hiking gear, backpacks, camping equipment) 
on a natural outdoor background with mountains. Modern, clean design with space on left 
for text. Professional product photography style. 1920x1080px.
```

## Banner 2: "Seasonal Offers" / "Special Deals"

```
E-commerce sale banner: Various products with discount tags, warm colors (orange/red/gold),
```


---

## General Tips for Image Generation:
1. **Text Space**: Always leave 30-40% of the left side clear for text overlay
2. **Colors**: Use brand colors (orange/black) or complementary colors
3. **Quality**: Generate at high resolution (1920x1080 minimum)
4. **Style**: Keep it consistent across all three banners
5. **Products**: Show actual products clearly but not cluttered
6. **Mood**: Each banner should have a distinct but cohesive feel
